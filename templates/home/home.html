About African OSS Spotlight

African OSS Spotlight is a community-driven initiative dedicated to increasing the visibility of open-source creators from across the African continent. Our mission is to build a comprehensive, public directory that showcases the incredible talent, innovative projects, and vibrant communities shaping the future of technology.

We believe that by highlighting these contributions, we can foster collaboration, inspire the next generation of developers, and connect African creators with global opportunities.

How It Works

Getting featured is simple. We want to make it as easy as possible for creators to join the directory and for the community to discover them.

edit_document

1. Submit Your Profile

Fill out a quick form with your details, including your name, location, social links (GitHub, Twitter, etc.), and a brief bio.

approval_delegation

2. We Review & Verify

Our team reviews each submission to ensure it meets our community guidelines and verifies the open-source contributions.

3. Get Discovered!

Once approved, your profile goes live! You'll be featured in our directory, visible to a global audience of peers and potential collaborators.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to be featured? expand_more

Any open-source creator of African descent, whether living on the continent or in the diaspora, is eligible. The key is active contribution to open-source projects.

Is there a cost to be listed? expand_more

No, absolutely not. This is a free, community-driven platform. There will never be a charge to be featured in the directory.
